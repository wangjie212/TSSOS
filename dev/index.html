TSSOS

TSSOS is a sparse polynomial optimization package based on the sparsity adapted moment-SOS hierarchies, which can fully exploit the sparsity in the problem data including correlative (variable) sparsity and term sparsity.

Authors

  • Jie Wang, Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

Installation

TSSOS is simply installed by running

pkg> add https://github.com/wangjie212/TSSOS

Related packages

  • DynamicPolynomials: Polynomial definition
  • MultivariatePolynomials: Polynomial manipulation
  • NCTSSOS: Noncommutative polynomial optimization
  • ChordalGraph: Chordal graphs and chordal extentions
  • SparseJSR: Computing joint spetral radius
